# Code of Conduct

TreasureRun welcomes respectful collaboration.

## Expected behavior

Please:

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent
- explain suggestions clearly
- keep discussions focused on the project
- be patient with contributors from different experience levels and language backgrounds

## Unacceptable behavior

The following is not acceptable:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- intentionally disruptive behavior
- sharing private information without permission
- hostile or insulting comments

## Reporting

If you notice a problem, please open a GitHub issue or contact the maintainer through the repository.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and constructive.
